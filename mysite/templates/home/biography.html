About Me

Throughout my career, I have leveraged my deep exposure to online publishing, streaming media, web applications development, and back-end services integration in the entertainment industry.

Most recently at CBS Interactive, I have assisted in the integration of back-end web services and front-end applications in AWS computer environments; developed web applications for content management systems; implemented optimization solutions to improve web page loading; and automated processes for the acquisition and processing of high volume media assets for online publication from content TV partners.

During my time at the Walt Disney Studios and Sony Pictures Entertainment, I was involved with the development of media work flows, processes automation, and applications integration for the production of animated feature films employing motion capture technology in 3D. Produced feature films that I have worked on include Body of Lies, Valkyrie, A Christmas Carol and Mars needs Moms.

While in graduate school at Harvard, I was given the opportunity to work and develop innovative production environments for streaming media and web content delivery for on-line education. Being one of the first adopters of early streaming media technologies allowed me to team up with industry development teams to build effective web based frameworks for online publishing and media distribution.
