My Work @ Disney

ImageMovers Digital is a division of the Walt Disney Studios run by Director Robert Zemeckis which specializes in the production of animated films employing motion capture and digital film technology in stereoscopic format ( also knows as 3D).

From a software development perspective, my work in the studio focused on the architecting of frameworks to define and optimize media production workflows. Specifically, I developed applications tools, automated large systems enclosures for the rendering of photorealistic image plates, image block segmentation, image reconstructions and image sequences, and implemented new features in applications employing their SDKs. Additionally, I developed dynamic Web apps and interfaces for the monitoring and debugging of production processes.

During my time at Disney, I was involved in the production of two feature films: A Christmas Carol and Mars Needs Moms.
