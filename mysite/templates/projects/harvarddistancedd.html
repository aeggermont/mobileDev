Distance Learning @ Harvard

During my time at Harvard I was given the opportunity to develop and implement a prototypical model and Web base framework for the production and publishing of online media content for online educational programs. This implementation represented an initiative to standardize media work flows and a systems architecture that would allow Harvard University Division of Continuing Education promote and continue further development in distance education technologies over the Internet as well as its expansion.

Specifically I developed a content management system for the dynamic generation of Web content, compression and transcoding of video streams, designed front-end Web interfaces with embedded streaming video and audio controls, synchronization of multimedia component, and automation of processes for content publishing.
